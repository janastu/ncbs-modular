SPACE TOUR

Space Tour offers a mash-up of oral histories, archival documents and contemporary spatial mapping techniques. The current tour is Obaid Siddiqi's office and lab, with the memories of Deepti Trivedi, a former student. Click on the information icon and arrows to navigate. Future developments will layer other people's memories and will be tours of other places.

ORAL HISTORY

Oral History Metadata Synchronizer is a widget to synchronize an interview transcript with the original oral history interview. Click on the timestamps to go directly to particular sections. In future versions of this widget, one could edit the transcript and encode annotations into an audio file. This is a sample of an interview of Obaid Siddiqi conducted by Indira Chowdhury where he discusses his entry into neurobiology.
